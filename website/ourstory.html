our story

How we got
here today

Founded in 1999, Enabled started as a cross-media company and evolved within the digital space. We sailed through the “Dot Com Bust”, then a decade later, seized the first mover advantage in mobile and touch technology.

But, if we had simply jumped onto anything just because it was in vogue, you would not be reading this today.

The speed of change is a litmus test of a clear vision amid ambiguity, e.g. picking the winning tech from the fad. Going through the uncertain has taught us how to spot and manage digital disruption.

Now we want to help you do the same.
