How we work (and play)

Our culture is what elevates individual brilliance to a whole new level.

  • Inspire and be inspired

    Our people are champions of their own craft and they can inspire others in this belief. As a multidisciplinary team, we have a desire to gain an appreciation of other disciplines and how our crafts can be combined together to achieve a greater whole.

  • Nuturing Creativity

    As a big family, we don’t let seniority get in the way of learning from each other. By constantly considering different perspectives, we subconsciously seek to improve the quality of our output and find new ways of solving problems.

  • Get the job done

    After identifying our client’s job to be done, we get it done, no matter what. Our close-knit team knows how to work in harmony and still challenge each other if needed to ensure we stay on the right track.

  • Be human

    Led by passion, guided by values, excited by innovation. While that is true, we also consume coffee like water, have hotdog fridays, cut Slack some puns. Working in tech, we strive to act like humans, not robots (most of the times).
