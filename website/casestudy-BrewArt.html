BrewArt

A new era in personal brewing has arrived. Technology, style and convenience for home brewing enthusiasts.

Craft your masterpiece

Let us turn your attention to BrewArt - the world’s first fully automated beer brewing machine.

Working closely with our client over the last 18 months, we took the original BrewArt prototype further by developing an Internet of Things feature.

Enabled built the accompanying mobile app that can talk to and automate the beer brewing machine.

To make this a fully automatic unit, we also embedded digital instructions in beer recipes and enabled integration with all the backend systems (e.g. eCommerce).


Aesthetics meet function

BrewArt provides a seamless beer brewing experience, backed by Omni-channel Retail. Enabled also worked on the User Experience, including artwork design (digital and offline), and animation production.

Stay tuned for future updates.
